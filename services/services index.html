#1 Recital Recording & Archiving

Hi-Definition A/V Production for Live Performances


About
This service is designed to provide an audience-perspective recording of the music scene.
High-quality audio and video recording system will be set to capture accurate sound and clear, durable images. At the same time, they will be placed discreetly to minimize interference with the audience's view.
After your recital, the records will be sent into professional editing system, which consists of specialized software with hi-fi playback system. After that, they will turn into a set of audio/video digital archive.

Price
$120/Hour*
*the duration of recital, w/ intermission.
*post-production process is included in service. No extra charges.
*Partial time within less than an hour is counted as one hour, and there's a five-minute grace period, so if it's within an hour and five minutes, it's still considered as one hour.


#2 Pre-Screen & Recorded Audition

Production of Recordings in Response to Institutions' Requests


About
This service is designed to provide audio and video profiles in response to the requirements in some application process. Typically they require a single shot with no obstructions ......
High-quality audio and video recording and editing systems are applied. At the same time, the editing system allows a real-time playback, it means that you can check your audio on the spot to quickly pick out the parts you want, which boosting efficiency and lowering expenses.

Price
$100/Hour*
*w/ break.
*post-production process is included in service. No extra charges.
*Partial time within less than an hour is counted as one hour, no grace period.
*Case by case. Group reservations is not permitted for now.
